How to Play Connect Four

Connect Four is a two-player game where the goal is to connect four of your own colored chips in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.

Players take turns dropping their colored chips from the top into a column on the game board. The chips fall to the lowest available position within the column.

The game ends when one player successfully connects four chips in a row or when the game board is filled with chips without a winner.
